DOCTOR ALLAMA IQBAL

THE POET OF EAST

Muhammad iqbal, also known as allama iqbal, is the national poet of pakistan. Allama iqbal was born on 9 november 1877 in sialkot and died on 21 april 1938. Iqbal completed his BA and MA at government collee Lahore and taught arabic at oriental college Lahore from 1899 to 1903. He is most renowned for his poetic composition including "Asrar-e-Khudi", "Rumuz-e-Bekhudi" and "Bang-e-Dara".
